Kielbasa that Penn likes

Ingredients

  • Kielbasa
  • Rice
  • Chicken Broth
  • Bell Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Heat up your pan & oil
  2. Slice the kielbasa, cook until pleasantly browned
  3. Probably get your rice cooking first.
  4. Slice up your vegetables, cook them, too.
  5. Same pan as the meat is good.
  6. Use the seasonings to season things.
  7. At some point prior to serving, combine the rice, meat, and vegetables.
  8. Eat it?
